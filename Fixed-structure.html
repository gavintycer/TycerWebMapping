My Favorite Fishing Spot:

This spot is in Chambers County, Texas close to a small town called Winnie. Here you can catch Alligator Gar, Catfish, Drum, Snapper, and more!

My Second Favorite Fishing Spot:

This spot is in Hays County, Texas near Wimberley. This is an area my family has been going to for for 70 years and is where I started fishing using a simple Tenkara Rod.

My Third Favorite Fishing Spot:

This area is very close to home in Lane County, Oregon! I call this area fall creek and its excellent for year round trout fishing and amazing summer swimming.
